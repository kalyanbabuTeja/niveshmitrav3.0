Chief Minister’s Message

"My government is fully committed to facilitate the investors and entrepreneurs for establishing and smoothly carrying out their business operations in the state. Nivesh Mitra, the single window portal, is one of the step in this direction. Through this dedicated single window we will ensure online clearances /approvals / NOCs, of all the departments which are required to set up and run the business in the State without any physical touch points. A centralised monitoring system, directly under my office, aims to deliver investors high quality and ever more responsive services in a time bound manner."
